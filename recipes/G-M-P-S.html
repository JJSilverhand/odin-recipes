This is a nice light soup that will nourish you completely.

Ingredients

  • Bone or Veggie Broth
  • Ginger
  • Coconut Milk
  • Miso Paste
  • Purple Sweet Potato
  • Chard or Spinich
  • Lemon

Instructions

  • Saute the Ginger in a tiny bit of sesame oil.
  • Add potato.
  • Add Broth.
  • Sift miso paste through a small strainer.
  • Bring to a boil and then let simmer until ginger and potatos are soft.
  • Add greens.
  • Squeeze in lemon and add coconut milk to taste.
