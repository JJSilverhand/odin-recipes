Peanut Butter Soy Sauce Chicken is a deliscious recipe that is easy to make and usually only takes 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up the sesame oil in the pan
  2. Put chicken in pan and add soy sauce and peanut butter to taste
  3. Add a little water if you like chicken more saucy
  4. Cook chicken for about 20-25 minutes on medium
